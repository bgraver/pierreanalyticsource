About This Website

This website, inspired by BaseballSavant, is a resource for advanced hockey statistics. You can find percentile rankings, live game data, and leaderboard data on this website.

Data

The data presented to you is pulled from the NHL's API. All advanced stat calculations are done by me and uploaded to a SQL database that this website pulls from. This website is an Angular frontend with an Express JS backend that pulls from a SQLite3 database. All player card statistics are calculated as a rate (per/60) and are percentile based.

Glossary

xGF:

The amount of expected goals for that a skater is on the ice for.

xGA:

The amount of expected goals for that a skater is on the ice against.

Finishing:

I calculated finishing as what is usually referred to as "Goals Scored Above Expected," which is simply the amount of goals a skater has scored subtracted by the amount of expected goals they have generated through shots themselves.

Primary Assists:

Assists where the skater was the last one on the scorer's team to touch or pass the puck before the scorer obtains possession (assuming no possession loss before this) and scores.

EV:

Even Strength (5v5, 4v4, 3v3)

PP:

Powerplay (Skater advantage due to penalty, 5v4 for example)

SH:

Shorthanded (Skater disadvantage due to penalty, 4v5 for example)

TOI:

Time On Ice
